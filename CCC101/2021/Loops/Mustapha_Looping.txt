Q. How do you use a "for" loop to iterate over a range of numbers from 0 to 3 and print each number?

A. for num in range(4):
print(num)


2. 
Q. How do you write a "for" loop to iterate over a list of numbers and print each number?

A. numbers = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
for num in numbers:
print(num)
